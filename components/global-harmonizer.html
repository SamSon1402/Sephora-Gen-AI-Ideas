How It Works

Our Global Team Harmonizer leverages several data sources:

  • Team member locations and time zones
  • Cultural working patterns by region
  • Historical meeting participation data
  • Regional holiday and time-off schedules
  • Individual working hour preferences

The AI identifies optimal collaboration windows that minimize disruption across all time zones and suggests fair rotations for unavoidable off-hours meetings.

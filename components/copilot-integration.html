How It Works

Our Copilot Integration extends Microsoft's AI capabilities with Sephora-specific knowledge and data:

  • Custom AI plugins for Sephora business processes
  • Secure connection to internal knowledge bases
  • Organization-specific prompt engineering
  • Role-based AI assistants for different departments
  • Governance and usage analytics dashboard

The system provides contextual assistance within Outlook, Teams, Word, Excel, PowerPoint and other Microsoft 365 applications.

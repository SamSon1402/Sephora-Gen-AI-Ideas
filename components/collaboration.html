How It Works

Our system creates a digital map of collaboration patterns by analyzing:

  • Teams channel communications
  • Document co-authoring relationships
  • Meeting participation networks
  • Knowledge sharing flows
  • Project tool interactions

The AI identifies both collaboration strengths and potential gaps, suggesting specific actions to improve team synergy.

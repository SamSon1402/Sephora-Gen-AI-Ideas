Overview

The Team Energy Heatmap is an AI-powered visualization tool that analyzes and displays the optimal meeting times for global teams across multiple time zones. By leveraging the Microsoft Graph API, we collect anonymized activity data to determine when team members are most alert, productive, and available.

How It Works

Our algorithm analyzes several data points from Microsoft 365 to create an accurate energy profile for each team member:

  • Calendar activity patterns
  • Email and Teams message time distributions
  • Document editing peak times
  • Self-reported productivity data
  • Meeting participation metrics

The system identifies optimal "golden hours" when team members across different regions are most likely to be at peak energy levels.

Microsoft Integration

This feature integrates directly with Microsoft Teams and Outlook to provide actionable meeting time suggestions when scheduling. The system automatically suggests optimal meeting times that respect everyone's energy patterns, not just their availability.

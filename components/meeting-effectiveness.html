How It Works

The Meeting Effectiveness tool analyzes Microsoft Teams and Outlook data to identify optimization opportunities:

  • Participation levels and engagement patterns
  • Meeting frequency and attendee overlap
  • Action item completion rates
  • Meeting duration vs. productive time
  • Calendar congestion patterns

The system then provides specific recommendations to improve meeting effectiveness.

Smart Meeting Recommendations

Example: Redundant Meeting Pattern
"Your team holds three separate status meetings with 80% overlapping attendees. Consider consolidating into a single weekly meeting with a structured agenda."
Example: Duration Optimization
"Product review meetings consistently end 15 minutes early. Consider scheduling for 45 minutes instead of 60 to free up calendar time."
